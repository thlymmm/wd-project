About Our Company

Welcome to Ann Yang Trading. We believe that every successful enterprise and project begins with a rock-solid foundation. Since our establishment on 5th April 2001 in Batu Pahat, Johor, Ann Yang Trading has consistently adhered to the core philosophy of "Integrity First, Quality Supreme" within the trading and supply industry.

With over 20 years of extensive industry experience, we have grown from a passionate startup serving Muar into a premium, deeply trusted business partner supplying tree seedlings and flowers across Kuala Lumpur, Johor Bahru, and southern Malaysia. We don't just supply exceptional horticultural products; we specialize in catering comprehensive supply chains and tailored solutions to help our local and international wholesale clients flourish.

Our main cultivation hub is strategically nested at Lot 1748, PLT 59, Jln Kg Lubok Tengah Semerah, Batu Pahat. Utilizing the ideal micro-climate and natural shade provided under oil palm trees, our resilient seedlings are optimally shielded from harsh direct sunlight, resulting in healthier, premium quality stocks for nursery operators and landscaping projects.

Our Featured Showcase

We cultivate hundreds of varieties catering to small and large volume wholesale orders with robust bulk discounts. Our top-selling botanical species include:

  • Alpinia purpurata (Elegant Red Ginger)
  • Piper sarmentosum (Wild Betel Leaf)
  • Pseuderanthemum 'golden' (Golden False Eranthemum)
  • Premium selections from the Euphorbiaceae, Polygonaceae, and Acanthaceae plant families.
